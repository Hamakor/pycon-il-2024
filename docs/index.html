About PyCon Israel

PyCon Israel 2024 will include a day of talks at Cinema City, Gelilot, and a day of workshops (location to be announced). Two days of learning and socializing, fun and collaboration, all around Python.

PyCon Israel has been taking place since 2016; this is a talk from the 2023 edition. In 2023 we had a day of talks and a day of workshops; videos of the talks are available.

For more details, visit the 2023 conference website.
